Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Legend ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Summary (TL;DR)

  • What we collect: Name, age, preferences, goals, usage data
  • Why: To personalize quotes and improve the App
  • Storage: Mostly on YOUR device (encrypted)
  • Sharing: Only with essential service providers (RevenueCat, Apple, Google AI)
  • Your rights: Access, update, delete your data anytime
  • Security: Industry-standard encryption and protection
  • Payments: Handled securely by Apple (we never see your payment info)
  • We DON'T: Sell your data, show ads, or track you for marketing

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you use Legend, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide during onboarding and app usage:

  • Name - Your first name for personalization
  • Age Range - Your age bracket (13-17, 18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-54, 55+)
  • Interests - Categories you select (e.g., Wealth, Fitness, Relationships, Travel, Family, Career)
  • Challenges - Personal challenges you're working on (e.g., Procrastination, Confidence, Discipline, Fear of failure, Focus, Stress)
  • Goals - Your personal goals and aspirations (optional text input)
  • Motivation Style - Your preference for motivational content (Encouraging or Alpha)
  • Notification Preferences - Your chosen notification frequency (Low, Medium, High) and opt-in status

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

  • Device Information - Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data - Features used, time spent in app, quotes viewed and favorited
  • App Interactions - Button clicks, screen views, navigation patterns
  • Technical Data - App crashes, performance data, error logs

1.3 Subscription Information

When you subscribe to Legend Premium, we collect:

  • Subscription Type - Monthly or Annual plan
  • Subscription Status - Active, expired, or cancelled
  • Purchase Receipts - Transaction IDs and purchase validation data (managed by Apple/RevenueCat)

Note: We do NOT store your payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple's App Store and RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Personalization

  • Generate AI-powered motivational quotes tailored to your interests, challenges, goals, and motivation style
  • Customize your content feed based on your preferences
  • Address you by name for a personalized experience

2.2 App Functionality

  • Schedule and send motivational notifications based on your chosen frequency
  • Save your favorite quotes for easy access
  • Manage your subscription status and access to premium features

2.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App
  • Identify and fix technical issues, bugs, and crashes
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Optimize performance and user experience

2.4 Customer Support

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Troubleshoot technical problems
  • Verify subscription status

2.5 Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users

3. How We Store Your Information

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your information is stored locally on your device using secure encrypted storage:

  • Your name, age, interests, challenges, goals, motivation style, and notification preferences
  • Your favorite quotes
  • Your onboarding completion status

This data is stored using React Native AsyncStorage with encryption and is NOT transmitted to our servers except when generating AI quotes.

3.2 Cloud Storage

Limited information is transmitted to our servers only for:

  • AI Quote Generation - Your interests, challenges, goals, motivation style, and age range are sent to our API to generate personalized quotes (powered by Google Gemini AI)
  • Subscription Management - Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

4.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Purpose: Manage in-app subscriptions and purchases
  • Data Shared: Device ID, purchase receipts, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4.2 Apple App Store

  • Purpose: Process subscription payments
  • Data Shared: Apple ID, payment information (managed by Apple)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4.3 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Generate personalized motivational quotes
  • Data Shared: Age range, interests, challenges, goals, motivation style (NO personally identifiable information)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.4 Expo / React Native

  • Purpose: App framework and infrastructure
  • Data Shared: Crash reports, performance metrics
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

4.5 Vercel (API Hosting)

  • Purpose: Host our quote generation API
  • Data Shared: API request data (interests, challenges, motivation style - NO PII)
  • Privacy Policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

With third-party service providers (listed in Section 4) who assist in operating the App and providing services.

5.2 Legal Requirements

If required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Protect user safety

5.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

6. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the App or clear its data
  • Server Data: Quote generation requests are processed in real-time and are NOT permanently stored
  • Subscription Data: Retained by RevenueCat for the duration of your subscription plus 1 year for refund/support purposes
  • Deleted Account: If you delete the App, all locally stored data is immediately removed

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access - View all data stored locally in the App settings
  • Update - Modify your preferences at any time in "Preferences" section
  • Delete - Remove all data by deleting the App or using "Reset Account" in settings
  • Export - Request a copy of your data (contact us at support@legendmotivation.com)

7.2 Notification Control

You can:

  • Opt out of notifications in the App's Notification settings
  • Adjust frequency (Low, Medium, High) at any time
  • Disable notifications completely in iOS Settings → Legend

7.3 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do NOT sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

7.4 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@legendmotivation.com

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Encryption - Data stored on your device is encrypted
  • Secure Transmission - All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Minimal Data Collection - We collect only what's necessary for functionality
  • No Payment Data Storage - Payment info is handled exclusively by Apple
  • Regular Security Audits - We review our practices regularly

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Legend is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@legendmotivation.com, and we will delete it immediately.

For Ages 13-17: Parental consent may be required depending on your jurisdiction. We encourage parents to monitor their children's app usage.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States, where our servers and service providers are located.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Privacy Shield frameworks (where applicable)
  • Consent for data transfers

11. Push Notifications

Legend sends motivational quote notifications based on your preferences:

  • Permission Required - You must grant notification permission
  • Frequency Control - You choose Low (1-2/day), Medium (3-5/day), or High (6-8/day)
  • Opt-Out Anytime - Disable in App settings or iOS Settings
  • No Marketing - Notifications contain ONLY motivational quotes, no ads or promotions

12. AI-Generated Content

Legend uses Google Gemini AI to generate personalized motivational quotes:

  • Data Sent to AI: Age range, interests, challenges, goals, motivation style
  • NO Personal Identifiers: We do NOT send your name, email, or device ID to AI services
  • One-Way Processing: Requests are processed in real-time and not stored by us
  • Google's Policies: Subject to Google's AI usage and privacy policies

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification (for material changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
